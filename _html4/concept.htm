ABOUT FORTUNE SUMMONERS



Fortune Summoners is a single-player real-time RPG with a unique, party-based sidescrolling battle system.

You will form a party of three brave young adventurers, and the party members not currently under your direct control will be managed by Lizsoft's "FOR-D" AI system.

Computer-controlled characters will follow the player character, navigating obstacles as you do, and when foes approach, they will enter the formation you determine and fight in the way you indicate. Sword fighters will protect spellcasters, and spellcasters will support those protecting them - or burn all the foes before them!


Cooperation is the key to unlocking the secret of the elemental stone. Do you have enough faith in your friends to see you through to the end? We'll find out...
