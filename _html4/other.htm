Adventuring Tips
Helping Out
Arche's father runs the item store in Tonkiness. If you are controlling Arche, you have the option of asking him if you can help out around the store.

Bringing Mr. Plumfield what he asks for adds more items for him to sell in the store, including things you can't get anywhere else... and it attracts more customers!

Storage Box
Arche keeps a convenient storage box in her room. If you have items in your inventory that you want to save but won't use any time soon, you can place them here for safekeeping.

You may also find storage boxes in other towns, and items placed into one box can be removed from any box.
Messages
From time to time, Arche and her friends will write themselves some notes to keep track of what's going on, and you may also receive mail from others. You may keep track of all this in the Messages section of the Main Menu.



If you have an unread message, you'll see this mark in the lower left of the screen.


Magic Rock Paper Scissors
Various kids throughout Scotsholm, particularly at Arche's school, may challenge you to a game of Magical Rock Paper Scissors, once you learn the rules from a classmate.

If you win enough times in a row, your opponent may give you a prize!
Capsule Machines
In many stores throughout the land, you will find Capsule Machines. If you put money in, you'll receive a capsule which can contain a variety of prizes. Some of these can only be found in Capsule Machines, so give it a try if you have money to spare.
Bonus Menu: Versus Mode
Versus Mode allows you to pick a character at a level of your choosing and have them fight against another, AI character in a duel. As you proceed through the main game, Versus Mode will unlock, as will more character and level options. Versus Mode may be accessed through the Bonus Menu on the title screen.

Bonus Menu: Auto-Play Mode
In Auto-Play Mode, you can watch a party of AI character challenge a dungeon. Auto-Play Mode may be accessed through the Bonus Menu on the title screen.
