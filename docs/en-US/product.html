What To Expect In The Full Version
In the full version, Stella will (after some cajoling) join the party, filling out your roster, and you'll have access to the strongest spells and sword techniques, such as Explosion, Water Blow, and The Full Moon.

Of course, the enemy won't hold back either, as your foes will form groups of warriors and mages, driven by the same AI which guides your party members!

The strategy behind these encounters is the real fun of Fortune Summoners, and we hope you'll give the full version a try!
